The impact of Computer Science and Technology on Communication/h1>

Technology has turned the world into a global village. Thanks to technology we can communicate with anybody of our choosing. We can communicate with people on the other side of the world with ease. Technology has developed the way we communicate with each other.

  • Stronger Relationships: Technology has strengthtned our relationships. We can constantly keep in touch with people far and near us. Due to this long distance relationships dont break down as easily as before.
  • E:Schools: Technology has also improved the educational system. In this trying times, School lectures are still going on due to video conferencing and other ways and means. Therefore
  • Jobs: Improvement of communication has also provided numerous jobs over the years. From jobs in ISPs and mobile phone manufactures and repaires, the number of jobs improved communication has provided is enoormous.
